Privacy Notice

What Personal Information About Customers Is Collected?

We collect various types of personal information to deliver services efficiently, personalize user experiences, and ensure secure transactions. This information helps enhance customer service, product recommendations, and communication.

  • Information You Give Us: Details provided during account creation, order placement, product reviews, customer service interactions, or profile updates.
  • Automatic Information: Data such as browsing history, device details, IP address, interaction with content and advertisements, and purchase behavior.
  • Information from Other Sources: Updated delivery and address details from carriers or other third parties to correct records and deliver purchases efficiently.

For What Purposes Is Your Personal Information Used?

  • Purchase and delivery of products and services, including order processing, payment handling, and delivery logistics.
  • Provide, troubleshoot, and improve services by analyzing performance and resolving issues.
  • Recommendations and personalization based on browsing and purchase history.
  • Voice, image and camera services for features like virtual assistants and smart devices.
  • Compliance with legal obligations such as regulatory requirements and fraud investigations.
  • Communication through emails, messages, or app notifications regarding orders, promotions, and support.
  • Advertising tailored to user interests, on and off the platform.
  • Fraud prevention and credit risk management to protect users and the platform from misuse.

What About Cookies and Other Identifiers?

Cookies, pixels, and tracking technologies are used to identify your browser or device. These tools help recognize returning users, remember preferences, and improve browsing experience.

Is Your Personal Information Shared?

  • Transactions involving Third Parties: Information may be shared with sellers, suppliers, and delivery partners during purchases.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: Other companies may perform tasks like data analysis, marketing, or payment processing.
  • Business Transfers: Personal information may be transferred as part of mergers, acquisitions, or sales of assets.
  • Protection of Users and Others: Information may be released when legally required or necessary to protect rights and safety.

How Secure Is Information About Me?

Security measures like encryption, secure servers, and multi-factor authentication are used to protect your personal data from unauthorized access.

What About Advertising?

Third-party advertisers display relevant ads. Users can manage their advertising preferences through account settings to limit or opt out of personalized advertising.

What Information Can I Access?

Through your account, you can view and update personal details such as name, address, payment methods, order history, and communication settings.

What Choices Do I Have?

You can manage your information by controlling cookies, adjusting communication preferences, and deciding what data to share or restrict.

Are Children Allowed to Use These Services?

Children under the age of 18 may only use services with the involvement of a parent or guardian. Personal data from minors is not knowingly collected.
