Sales and Refunds

General Returns Policy

Applicable products are returnable within the applicable return window if you've received them in a condition that is physically damaged, has missing parts or accessories, defective, or different from their description on the product detail page.

If you report an issue with your fresh fruit or produce order, we may facilitate scheduling a quality check or replacement delivery depending on your location. Service availability depends on the delivery area and policies of the fruit delivery service.

Returns will be processed only if:

  • It is determined that the product was damaged during transit or delivery;
  • The product is different from what was ordered;
  • The product is returned in its original condition, including proper packaging where applicable.

If you wish to return perishable items, please ensure they are handled carefully and returned promptly as freshness cannot be guaranteed after a certain period. The website shall not be liable for any spoilage or misuse of returned products.

Some products may not be eligible for return as covered in detail in the “Return Policy Types” table below, including cases of buyer's remorse such as ordering the wrong variety or quantity of fruit.

Products marked as "non-returnable" on the product detail page cannot be returned. However, in the unlikely event of receiving damaged, defective, or incorrect fruit items, we will provide a full refund or replacement as applicable. We may contact you to verify the issue before issuing a refund or replacement.

No additional information is required to return an eligible order unless otherwise noted in category-specific policies.

Products may be eligible for replacement only if the same seller has the exact same items in stock. If the seller does not have the exact same product available, a refund will be issued instead of a replacement.

Products purchased by international customers are not eligible for returns. However, international customers may be eligible for refunds and should contact customer service within 5 business days from the delivery date or estimated delivery date to claim refunds.

For domestic customers, please refer to the product display page for specific replacement or return time windows before purchase.

In the event customers are found to misuse the return policy by excessively returning, cancelling, or not accepting orders placed, the website reserves the right to warn, suspend, block, or terminate such customer accounts as necessary.

Note: If you receive a non-returnable product in damaged or defective condition, please contact us within 5 days from delivery.

Note: All product categories are non-returnable for international customers on export orders.
